# Usage

This solution pack enables Security Operations Center (SOC) teams to automate the triage, ticketing, and reporting of threat intelligence alerts generated by FortiRecon’s **External Attack Surface Management (EASM)**, **Brand Protection (BP)**, and **Adversary Centric Intelligence (ACI)** modules.

By leveraging prebuilt playbooks, the solution reduces manual workload and response time, while improving consistency and visibility across incidents.

## Ticketing Automation

The **Ticketing Automation** playbooks help SOC teams manage FortiRecon findings by automatically generating tickets in systems like JIRA. These playbooks operate across EASM, BP, and ACI modules, following a standardized process:

### General Workflow

1. Trigger from a FortiRecon record (e.g., a Typosquat domain alert or brand misuse event).
2. Extract key metadata from the record and format it into an HTML table.
3. Invoke the **Create JIRA Ticket** utility playbook to raise a ticket with structured threat data.

**Example:**
`Create Ticket for Typosquat Domain Threat Alerts` playbook automates the response to domain spoofing alerts by generating a JIRA ticket with actionable context for analysts.

## Intelligence Reporting

The **Reporting** playbooks assist with periodic threat intelligence dissemination. These playbooks retrieve structured data from ACI modules and email CSV reports to stakeholders. They are organized into thematic collections:

- **APT Intelligence**
- **CERT Advisories**
- **Malware Intelligence**
- **Ransomware Intelligence**
- **Strategic Threat Intelligence**

### General Workflow

1. Pull relevant data based on a specified input (e.g., *Adversary Name*).
2. Format and extract key insights from the report.
3. Use the **Send Email Notification** utility playbook to send the report as a CSV attachment.

**Example:**
The `Get Intelligence Reporting on APT Group` playbook automatically compiles APT group-related data and delivers it to selected recipients via email.
