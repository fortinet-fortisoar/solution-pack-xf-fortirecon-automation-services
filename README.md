# Overview

The **FortiRecon Automation Services** solution pack provides automated playbooks to streamline incident response for *External Attack Surface Management (EASM)*, *Brand Protection (BP)*, and *Adversary Centric Intelligence (ACI)* use cases. It enables security analysts to efficiently manage *Critical* and *High-severity* issues by automating ticket creation in Jira and sending notifications for relevant services.

Each playbook follows a consistent workflow—triggering on active issues, retrieving metadata, formatting issue details into HTML, and generating tickets or alerts—enhancing SOC efficiency and ensuring timely, structured incident handling.

## Triage & Ticketing Automation

Eliminate manual handling of threat alerts. Prebuilt playbooks auto-generate structured tickets in tools like JIRA, so no alert slips through the cracks. From typosquatting to brand misuse, each event is captured, formatted, and escalated—instantly.

## Automated Intelligence Reporting

Never miss a strategic update. Curated playbooks pull intelligence on APTs, malware, ransomware, and more—delivering timely CSV reports directly to your stakeholders.

With consistency built in and response time slashed, your SOC gains the edge it needs to stay ahead of the threat curve.
